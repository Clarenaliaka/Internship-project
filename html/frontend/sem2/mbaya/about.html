About KonaMbaya

Who we are

We are a group of captivative developers and graphic designers from different institutions
who are passionate about giving I.T solutions to our country Kenya.
We mainly focus on what the Kenyans' citizen goes through on a
daily basis: like theft of devices and accidents on black spots while travelling
in different part of the countries.

What we have done

We have come up with an application called Kona Mbaya. Basically it is an application that deals with informing Kenyans' about places where their devices are prone to theft and the black spots on our roads.

The application uses the GPRS idea where it can update or alert people on the road black spots and the places where theft is at avery high rate. people inturn should take precautionary measures .

Our motivation

people focus so much about big problems and fail to remember small problems. So as a teamwe decided to give a solutin to the problems on the grass roots. In the end we make a better tommorrow.


Our vision

Kona Mbaya aim is to develop an application that will change Kenyan future.
